# MT Contributing Rules

Here you can learn **how to improve Muffin Text**, by reporting bugs and in the future, collaborating directly in the MT Devlopment and Ecosystem.

---

## Reporting Bugs

If you found a **Bug** in a MT project, the first thing to do is to open a *Issue* in the MT Repo. In the **[Issues Page](https://github.com/jhojho3jogos456-design/The-MT-Project/issues)**

### Writing the Issue

**Must Include:**

- The *Muffin Text Editor or Runtime version* that the bug affects.

- What is the Bug and how the bug appears.

**Opitional:**

- Media (Screenshot or video) showing the bug.
